Mutton Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric
  • Oil, salt, coriander

Cooking Process:

  1. Heat oil, sauté onions till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, then tomatoes and spices.
  3. Add mutton, cook till browned.
  4. Add water, pressure cook for 5–6 whistles.
  5. Garnish with coriander. Serve hot.
