Kichdi

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric, cumin, salt

Cooking Process:

  1. Wash rice and dal. Soak for 15 minutes.
  2. In a cooker, heat ghee, add cumin, then onion and tomato.
  3. Add soaked rice-dal, spices, and 3 cups water.
