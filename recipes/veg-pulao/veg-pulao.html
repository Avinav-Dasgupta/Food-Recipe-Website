Veg Pulao

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, beans, carrots)
  • 1 sliced onion
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cardamom, cloves)
  • Salt, ghee, water

Cooking Process:

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 mins.
  2. In pan, heat ghee, saut√© spices and onions.
  3. Add vegetables, cook slightly, then add rice and water (2:1 ratio).
  4. Cook covered on low heat till rice is done.
