Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut)
  • 2 onions, 2 tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices: chili powder, turmeric, garam masala
  • Oil, salt

Cooking Process:

  1. Saut√© onions in oil until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, then tomatoes and spices.
  3. Cook till oil separates, then add chicken pieces.
  4. Add water, cover and cook till chicken is tender.
  5. Serve with rice or roti.
