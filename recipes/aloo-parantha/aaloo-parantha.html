Aaloo Parantha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 medium boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 chopped green chilies
  • Salt, garam masala, coriander

Cooking Process:

  1. Prepare dough using flour and water; rest it.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices to create filling.
  3. Roll dough ball, stuff with filling, and roll again.
  4. Cook on tawa with ghee until golden on both sides.
